How I'm Managing COVID-19

By Samantha Brooker has always prided themselves on exceptionally high standards of hygiene. COVID-19 has meant adjustments but thankfully no major changes to the way in which we work. Sam has undertaken a qualification in COVID-19 hygiene and is committed to doing everything possible to reduce risk of transmission.

As a brief overview you will see the following changes:

  1. Client will be required to wear a face mask throughout their service and have a temperature check before any treatments can begin.
  2. Client must consent to having their details stored securely and be used for Test and Trace purposes should the need arise.
  3. Therapist will now be wearing the following â€“ Face shield, gloves, and disposable apron.
  4. Tools and implements will now be stored in individual boxes for individual clients. A deep soak Barbicide treatment will be used between each client.
  5. Family or friends will no longer be able to be present throughout the service to reduce the amount of people in one room. The exception to this is if you have a registered carer.
  6. Electronic payment will only be accepted, no cash.
