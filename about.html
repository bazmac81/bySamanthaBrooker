About Sam

With an extensive background within the beauty industry Sam has developed a great passion for helping women to feel great about themselves. The simplicity of an eyebrow wax or the de-fuzz ready for your summer holiday can have a huge impact on how we feel and Sam believes that her experience combined with carrying out your treatment in the comfort of your own home adds up to the perfect experience.

Sam has previously worked in corporate retail operating as a Head of Training and has worked in many different environments such as QVC, Professional Beauty, Fashion Week, Retail Stores and Salon Environments.

Specialising in brow shaping Sam has become an authority in all things brows ensuring you get the very best for you whilst keeping up to date with innovative techniques.

“I’ve always been fascinated how a single brow hair can have the ability to change the whole eye area. Everyone deserves to know what a great pair of brows can do for them!”
